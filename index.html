The Geothermal Food Processing Frontier

A Powerful Energy-to-Food Platform for Kenya and East Africa

EARTHCOOK transforms geothermal heat from the Great Rift Valley into affordable, low-carbon food processing for households, institutions, and regional supply chains.

Explore Strategy View Roadmap

Why This Matters Now (April 2026)

Energy Volatility

Diesel and LPG shocks are pushing food processing costs upward, while geothermal direct-use heat offers stable long-run pricing.

Food Security Pressure

Post-harvest losses and expensive cooking fuels reduce affordability of staples, especially for low-income urban households.

Climate & Forest Risk

Charcoal dependence drives deforestation and air pollution; geothermal processing can displace carbon-intensive cooking pathways.

The EARTHCOOK Model

Operational Value Chain

  1. Local Rift Valley sourcing.
  2. Preparation and quality-controlled pre-processing.
  3. Geothermal heat exchange for cooking/drying/pasteurization.
  4. Packaging, storage, and multi-channel distribution.

Product Portfolio

  • Institutional bulk catering.
  • Pre-boiled grains for food vendors.
  • Nutritional value packs for urban workers.
  • MREs for aid, defense, and mobility use-cases.
  • Fruit roll-ups from surplus produce.
